TWO MONTHS OF GAS MAINS REPAIRS ON ST MARGARETS ROAD
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    National Grid mains repairs will disrupt buses, parking and traffic
    for two months on St Margarets Road. Starting on 18 January from
    The Barrons to the A316 followed by works from the A316 to
    Netherton Road.
