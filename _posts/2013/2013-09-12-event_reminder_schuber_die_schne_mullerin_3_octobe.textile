_Will Morgan, tenor and John Paul Ekins, piano_

Two fabulous professional musicians are performing Schubert's song-cycle Die sch&#246;ne M&#252;llerin at "7.00 on Thursday October the 3<sup>rd</sup>":http://stmargarets.london/event/concert/200705144140 in the Richmond and Putney Unitarian Church, Ormond Road Richmond.

Before the performance there will be an illustrated talk on the work, and there will be a chance to discuss the work with the performers afterwards.  Tickets are &pound;7.50 on the door and refreshments will be available.
